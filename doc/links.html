4XMM-DR13 data tables

  • 4XMM_DR13cat_v1.0.fits.gz — full catalog in FITS format; recommended for more in-depth studies (includes information of all detections)
  • 4XMM_DR13cat_v1.0.csv.gz — same as above, but in CSV format
  • 4XMM_DR13cat_slim_v1.0.fits.gz — reduced version of the catalog in FITS format, suited for studies of unique sources (individual detection information is not included)
  • 4XMM_DR13cat_slim_v1.0.csv.gz — same as above, but in CSV format

4XMM-DR13 hips

4XMM-DR13 PN images have been merged in 4 Hips available on the SSC Hips node operated in Strasbourg:

  • Colored made from EP2,3,4 bands
  • low energy made from EP2 band images
  • medium energy made from EP3 band images
  • high energy made from the band images

TAP (Table Access Protocol) server

XMM catalogs are available through TAP services operated by Vizier.

  • Slim catalogs
    • IX/59/xmm4d9s
    • IX/59/xmm4d10s
    • IX/59/xmm4d11s
    • IX/59/xmm4d12s
    • IX/59/xmm4d13s
  • Stacked catalogs
    • IX/61/xmm4d9s
    • IX/64/xmm4d10s
    • IX/66/xmm4d11s
They can be accessed from various clients:
  • TAP Vizier
  • TapHandle
  • TOPCAT
  • Aladin
TAP server at CDS VizieR allows you to do large scale cross-matches of your source lists with the XMM-Newton catalog.

XCatDB

The SSC provides another Web interface based on SAADA and named the XCatDB (http://xcatdb.unistra.fr). Its main features are:

  • Access by source or by detection
  • All-sky browser based on Aladin Lite
  • Multi criteria search
  • Search by sky regions
  • Online spectrum fitting
  • Online SAS processing
  • Static correlation links with 220 archival catalogs (computed by the pipeline)

Stacked catalogs are not available in the XCatDB
